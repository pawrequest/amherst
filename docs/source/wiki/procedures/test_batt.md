# Testing Radio Batteries
Rechargeable batteries of all types do not last forever. 

After several hundred charge / discharge cycles, their storage capacity will reduce to a point where they need to be replaced.

Rechargeable battery packs for walkie-talkies will have information about the battery on a label on the back of the battery.

This label will state the type of battery (almost always Li-Ion or Li-Poly, both abbreviations for Lithium Ion) and the “nominal” capacity in mAh (milli-Amp-Hours).

## Cadex Battery Testers
We have 2 Cadex battery testers, one has 2 “slots” the other has 4 “slots”.
We have wired up each “slot” to a device that will hold a battery used by our hire or popular sale radios. 
We have also programmed the Cadex machines for the appropriate capacity for each battery.
