Daily Start-up Procedure

If you are first into the office, on arrival make sure that:

The front door is not double-locked

The office telephone is un-diverted.

Check for hire orders due out this day, or earlier ones that have not been sent or collected and get them ready to go.

Make sure that we are all “packed” ready for Parcelforce collection to avoid having to waste time driving parcels to Post Offices and Parcelforce depots later on in the day.

Pack any orders going out “same day” and arrange couriers as early in the day as is practical.
